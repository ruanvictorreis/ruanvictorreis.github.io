Ruan Reis

Biography

I am a Computer Science MSc student at Federal University of Campina Grande - Department of Computing Systems, Brazil. My research interest involves the study of program synthesis techniques and program visualization techniques. I am member of Software Productivity Group (SPG).

I am an enthusiastic software developer extremely passionate about technology. I am always trying to implement creative solutions to solve problems and to think outside of the box, which benefits both myself and my teammates. I love programming for pleasure and exploring knowledge in Android, IOS and other emerging technologies. I often spend my time on personal projects implementing my ideas, particularly in Python, Java, Swift(IOS) and Android.
